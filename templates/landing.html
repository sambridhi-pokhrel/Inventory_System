Features

Everything your inventory needs

Built for small businesses and academic institutions that need smart stock control without complexity.

AI Demand Forecasting

Linear Regression models trained on your sales history predict future demand and suggest optimal reorder quantities.

Real-time Dashboard

Live KPI cards, 7-day sales trends, and stock alerts — all visible the moment you log in.

Analytics & Reports

Monthly sales vs purchases, top products chart, revenue trends, and CSV export for deeper analysis.

Transaction Management

Record sales and purchases with payment gateway support. Every transaction feeds the AI model.

Role-based Access

Admin, Manager, and Staff roles with approval workflow. Google OAuth login with admin approval gate.

Smart Alerts

AI-powered reorder alerts appear on your dashboard when stock is predicted to run out before the next restock.

How it works

From signup to insights in minutes

No complex setup. Add your items, record transactions, and let the AI do the rest.

1

Create your account

Register with email or Google. Admin approves your access and assigns your role.

2

Add inventory items

3

Record transactions

Log every sale and purchase. The system tracks stock levels in real-time automatically.

The ML model learns your demand patterns and tells you exactly when and how much to reorder.
